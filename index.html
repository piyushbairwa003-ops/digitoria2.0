Company Story

When Kamal Vaswani looked at Ajmer in 2017, he didn't just see a city — he saw a gap. A gap between where local businesses stood and where they could be with the right digital strategy. What followed was not just the birth of a company. It was the beginning of a movement that would redefine digital marketing across Rajasthan.

2017 — When It Was Just a Dream

Before there was an office, before there were clients, before there was even a logo — there was a question: "What if Ajmer had a world-class digital marketing agency?"

That question came from Kamal Vaswani, a visionary who understood something most people in 2017 hadn't yet grasped: the internet wasn't the future of business — it was already the present. And Ajmer's businesses were missing out entirely.

At the time, Ajmer's digital landscape was sparse. Most businesses relied on word-of-mouth, print ads, and traditional hoardings. Social media was a hobby, not a strategy. SEO was a buzzword very few actually understood. The city had talent, ambition, and incredible businesses — but no one was telling their story online.

Kamal saw that gap and made a decision that would change everything. He chose to build the agency that Ajmer desperately needed — one that would compete not just locally, but nationally. That decision became Digitoria.

June 25, 2017 — The Day Digitoria Was Born

Mark the date. June 25, 2017.

On this day, Digitoria officially came into existence — not with a grand launch event or a boardroom full of investors, but with the quiet, fierce conviction of one person who believed Ajmer deserved better.

There were no clients yet. No team. No office. Just belief — and a crystal-clear vision of what Digitoria could become. That belief became the foundation everything else was built on. It became the reason 100+ businesses would eventually trust their SEO, Meta Ads, Google Ads, and website development to this one agency from Chandra Vardai Nagar, Ajmer.
